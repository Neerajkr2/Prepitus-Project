Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Prepitus and our services.

What is Prepitus and how does it work?
Prepitus is an educational technology platform that connects students with qualified tutors for personalized learning experiences. The platform uses smart matching algorithms to pair students with tutors based on their learning needs, subject requirements, and scheduling preferences.
How do I find the right tutor for my needs?
You can browse through our verified tutor profiles, filter by subject, experience level, rating, and availability. Our matching system also recommends tutors based on your specific learning goals and preferences to help you find the perfect match.
What subjects and levels does Prepitus cover?
Prepitus offers tutoring services for a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, Sciences, Languages, Humanities, and Test Preparation. We cover all educational levels from elementary school to university and professional certification courses.
How are the tutoring sessions conducted?
Tutoring sessions can be conducted online through our virtual classroom platform which includes interactive whiteboards, file sharing, and video conferencing. In-person sessions are also available based on location and tutor availability.
What is the pricing structure for tutoring services?
Pricing varies based on the tutor's experience, subject complexity, and session duration. You can view each tutor's hourly rate on their profile. We offer package deals for multiple sessions and have different payment options available.
How does Prepitus ensure the quality of tutors?
All tutors on our platform undergo a rigorous verification process including credential checks, background verification, and teaching assessments. We also maintain a rating and review system where students can provide feedback after each session.
